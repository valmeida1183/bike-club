More than just a store

Welcome to Bycicle Club, where passion meets craftsmanship. Since 1962, we have been dedicated to creating high-quality, customized bicycles that stand out for their performance, durability, and unique design. Founded by William Carter, a visionary cycling enthusiast, Bycicle Club has grown into a trusted brand for riders who seek more than just a bike—they seek an experience.

At Bycicle Club, we believe that a bicycle is more than just a means of transportation; it’s a lifestyle. That’s why we use only the finest materials and cutting-edge technology to build bikes that are tailored to your personal style and needs. Whether you're a casual rider or a seasoned cyclist, our collection of custom bicycles ensures that you ride with comfort, efficiency, and elegance.

Beyond our store, Bycicle Club is also a community. Our exclusive "Tour" service offers members the opportunity to explore the city through organized bike rides on scenic circuits. These tours provide not only an unforgettable cycling experience but also a chance to connect with fellow enthusiasts who share the same passion for adventure and freedom. Join Bycicle Club today and be part of a tradition that has been redefining the cycling world for over six decades. Ride with style, ride with purpose—ride with Bycicle Club.
